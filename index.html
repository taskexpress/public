G Task Express

What Our Extension Does with User Data

Our extension accesses your Google Tasks list data to provide a seamless experience for managing your tasks directly from your browser. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Create new tasks in your selected Google Task list
  • Access and display your Google Task lists

Enhancing Your Productivity

This extension enhances your productivity by allowing you to quickly create and manage tasks without leaving your current browser session. You can:

  • Use the extension popup to create a task with a title and notes
  • Right-click to create a task with selected text as the title or notes
  • Right-click to create a task with the webpage title as the task title and the webpage URL as the notes
  • Reload your Google Task lists in both the popup and right-click dropdown menu
